How Park City Booking Works

Booking a private SUV transfer with Rio Transportation takes about 2 minutes. Choose your route (SLC Airport ↔ Park City, or local Park City rides), pick a vehicle from our fleet of luxury 7-passenger SUVs, enter your flight number so we can track it, provide pickup details, and confirm. You'll receive an email confirmation immediately with your driver's name, vehicle details, and license plate number within 24 hours of your trip.

What's Included in Every Booking

Every Rio Transportation booking is all-inclusive, meaning no surprise fees at drop-off. Your price covers: the private luxury SUV (GMC Yukon, Lincoln Navigator, Cadillac Escalade, Nissan Pathfinder, or Chevrolet Suburban), a professional chauffeur with 5+ years of Utah mountain-driving experience, real-time flight tracking so we adjust pickup if your flight changes, bottled water and in-vehicle Wi-Fi hotspot, all fuel and road tolls, and one free intermediate stop (grocery store, rental shop, or ATM). Free car seats (infant, convertible, booster) are available on request.

Flight Tracking & Arrival Process

When you enter your flight number, our dispatch system begins tracking it via FlightAware about 24 hours before departure. If your flight is early, late, delayed, rerouted, or cancelled and rebooked, we automatically adjust your driver's arrival time — no action required from you. About 15-20 minutes before your estimated landing, your driver heads to the Salt Lake City Airport (SLC) ground-transportation curb. After you collect luggage, walk out of baggage claim at doors 5 or 11; your driver will text you the exact vehicle location and send a photo of the vehicle.

Cancellation & Changes

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your pickup time. Changes to pickup time, location, or passenger count within 24 hours of the ride are accommodated whenever possible at no cost. Cancellations within 24 hours are charged 50% of the fare; no-show cancellations are charged the full fare. If your flight is cancelled by the airline and you rebook for a later day, we rebook your ride for the new date at no charge.

Payment & Gratuity

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and corporate invoicing for accounts with $1,000+ monthly spend. Your card is charged after the ride completes so you can adjust tip amount. Gratuity is at your discretion — there is no mandatory gratuity and no hidden service fees. Most clients tip 15-20% for excellent service, but this is never required.

Booking Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For standard weekday trips: 24 hours is plenty. For ski-season weekends (Nov-Apr, Fri-Sun): 48-72 hours. For Sundance Film Festival (late January) or major holidays (New Year's, Christmas): 2-4 weeks ahead. Same-day bookings are usually accommodated if we have vehicles free — call (435) 214-6939 to check.

What if I'm not sure of my exact itinerary yet?

Book with your best estimate — you can modify the booking up to 24 hours before pickup at no charge. Many clients book a "placeholder" slot and finalize details a few days before travel.

Can I book a return trip later?

Yes — book round-trip by selecting both routes in the form, or book them separately. Many clients book arrival first, then book departure once flight times firm up.

Do you accept bookings without a credit card?

Credit card is required to secure the booking. Corporate accounts with NET-30 invoicing get booked without per-trip card charges; contact us for account setup.

What happens after I submit this booking form?

Within 5 minutes you receive a confirmation email. Within 30 minutes, you receive a second email with your assigned driver's name and vehicle. The day before your ride, you get a reminder email with exact pickup address/time. Day of the ride, your driver texts you their ETA.
