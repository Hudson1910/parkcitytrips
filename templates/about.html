About · Since 2016

The story
behind every ride.

Park City, Utah is a destination that rewards people who take its mountain roads slowly. We started Rio Transportation in 2016 with a single SUV and a simple promise: every guest gets the same care a regular gets. No shared shuttles, no surge pricing, no shrugged shoulders when a flight is late.

The fleet is now four tiers — Tesla Model Y for the eco-couples, Nissan Pathfinder for the families, GMC Yukon for the most-popular Premier tier, Cadillac Escalade for the VIP transfers — but the operating philosophy hasn't moved. Hudson and a small team of professional drivers cover every single trip personally.

Our drivers live on the mountain. They know which Stein Eriksen entrance to pull up to, which Deer Valley villa has a hidden ski valet, which switchback on Parley's gets icy first after a storm. That kind of local knowledge is the difference between getting somewhere and arriving somewhere.

Beyond airport transfers, we also handle hourly local rides (dining, shopping, resort hopping), ski-day chauffeur service, wedding party logistics, and corporate ski-group transportation. If you're moving more than eight people for an event, we coordinate multiple vehicles in sync.

The goal has always been the same. Make the ride feel like the first 45 minutes of vacation rather than the last 45 minutes of travel. Reserve early, especially during ski season and Sundance — and we'll see you at baggage claim.
