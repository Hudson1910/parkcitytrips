Why Rio Transportation

Rio Transportation LLC

Park City's Trusted Private Car Service Since 2019

Rio Transportation was founded with one simple mission: provide the safest, most reliable private car service between Salt Lake City Airport and Park City. What started as a one-vehicle operation has grown into a fleet of luxury SUVs serving thousands of passengers every year.

With 9,000+ rides completed, we know every route, every resort entrance, and every shortcut in the Wasatch Mountains. Our professional drivers are licensed, insured, background-checked, and trained specifically for Utah's winter road conditions.

We're not a rideshare or a shuttle bus — every ride is a private, door-to-door experience in a premium SUV. Real-time flight monitoring, complimentary water, phone chargers, and Wi-Fi come standard on every trip.
