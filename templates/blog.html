Park City, read in.

Notes, tips and insider knowledge from the Rio Transportation team — written by drivers who log thousands of miles a year on these mountain roads.

Welcome to the Park City Travel Guide — a curated collection of insider knowledge from our drivers, who log thousands of miles a year on Park City's roads and talk to visitors from around the world. Whether you're planning a first ski trip, a summer mountain-biking weekend, or a Sundance Film Festival visit, you'll find practical advice here from people who live and work in Park City every day.

Our articles cover seasonal topics (when to visit, what to pack, how weather affects your trip), ski-resort comparisons (Park City Mountain vs Deer Valley — which is right for your group?), dining and nightlife on Main Street, event coverage (Sundance logistics, summer festivals), and practical transportation guidance. Every post is written by our team, not AI-generated or scraped from other sites.

New posts are published monthly. If you have a specific Park City question that isn't covered here, contact us — our drivers answer questions all day long and are happy to share local knowledge by phone, text, or during your ride.
