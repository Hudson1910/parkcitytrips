Fleet questions

Are all vehicles AWD or 4WD?

Yes — every vehicle has all-wheel-drive (AWD) or four-wheel-drive (4WD), required for safe winter driving through Parley's Canyon and Park City's mountain roads. Tesla Model Y comes standard with electric AWD; the GMC Yukon and Cadillac Escalade have full 4WD with low-range gearing.

Do you have winter tires?

November through April, our entire fleet runs on dedicated winter tires (snow-rated, M+S certified for Utah Level 2 traction laws). May through October we run all-season tires. We never use summer tires — too dangerous for sudden winter storms in the Wasatch.

Can I request a specific vehicle?

Yes — at booking, choose the tier (Comfort Electric, Midsize, Premier, Luxury) and we assign a vehicle from that category. If you have a preference within the category (e.g. GMC Yukon over Chevy Suburban for Premier), note it in the booking and we'll honor it when possible.

How old are the vehicles?

Every vehicle in our fleet is under 3 years old. We rotate the fleet regularly to keep the ride experience consistent. Our Tesla Model Ys are 2025-2026 models; the GMC Yukons and Cadillac Escalades are similarly recent.

Premier vs Luxury — what's the difference?

Both seat 7 with similar luggage capacity. Premier (GMC Yukon) is our most popular tier — premium leather, captain's chairs, standard amenities. Luxury (Cadillac Escalade) adds privacy glass, heated/cooled seats, premium audio and a more refined ride. Choose Luxury for VIP transfers or special occasions; Premier for excellent everyday service.

Can I add car seats for my kids?

Yes — free of charge. We provide infant (rear-facing), convertible (toddler) and booster (4-10 years) seats. Just note the ages and weights when booking and we'll install the right seats before pickup.
