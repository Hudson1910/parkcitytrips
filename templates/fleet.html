Fleet FAQs

Are all vehicles AWD or 4WD?

Yes — all our vehicles have all-wheel-drive (AWD) or four-wheel-drive (4WD), required for safe winter driving through Parley's Canyon and Park City's mountain roads. Tesla Model Y comes standard with electric AWD; the GMC Yukon and Lincoln Navigator have full 4WD with low-range gearing.

Do you have winter tires?

November through April, our entire fleet runs on dedicated winter tires (snow-rated, M+S certified for Utah Level 2 traction laws). May through October we run all-season tires. We never use summer tires — too dangerous for sudden winter storms in the Wasatch.

Can I request a specific vehicle?

Yes — at booking, choose the tier (Comfort Electric, Midsize, Premier, Luxury) and we assign a vehicle from that category. If you have a preference within the category (e.g., GMC Yukon over Chevy Suburban for the Premier tier), let us know in the booking notes and we'll honor it when possible.

How old are the vehicles?

Every vehicle in our fleet is under 3 years old. We rotate the fleet regularly to ensure the best ride experience. Our Tesla Model Ys are 2025-2026 models; the GMC Yukons and Lincoln Navigators are similarly recent.

What's the difference between Premier and Luxury?

Both seat 7 with similar luggage capacity. Premier (GMC Yukon) is our most popular tier — premium leather, captain's chairs, all the standard amenities. Luxury (Lincoln Navigator or Cadillac Escalade) adds privacy glass, heated/cooled seats, premium audio system, and a more refined ride. Choose Luxury for VIP executive transfers or special occasions; Premier for excellent everyday service.

Can I add car seats for my kids?

Yes — free of charge. We provide infant (rear-facing), convertible (toddler), and booster (4-10 years) seats. Just let us know the ages and weights when booking and we'll install the right seats before pickup.
